Voxta

Your speech-driven AI companion backend

Natural conversations with AI characters

Talk with a virtual friend, have fun with a character, and interact with them in virtual reality. Unrestricted.

Bring your own AI

Supports multiple Large Language Model programs, such as:

  • KoboldAI
  • Oobabooga's Text Generation Web UI
  • Hugging Face's Text Generation Inference
  • OpenAI GPT-3.5 and GPT-4
  • OpenRouter
  • NovelAI

Speech

You can go with what works best for you and your budget.

  • Azure Speech Service
  • Windows Speech
  • NovelAI
  • ElevenLabs
  • Vosk

Multicultural

There are options for other languages, including speech transcription, speech synthesis, large language models, and character definitions.

Features

  • Tavern character cards support
  • Customizable prompts (Scriban templates)
  • AI text generation adapted for speech
  • Wake word support (Azure)
  • Peek inside the AI using "Analysis mode"
  • Keyword and summarization memory
  • Action inference: Avatars can act

Open source

While we will begin with Patreon-only users, the goal is for Voxta to be open source, and community-driven.

We hope to see many front-ends and contributors in the future!
