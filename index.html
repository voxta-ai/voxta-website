Voxta is a speech-driven AI companion backend.

Whether you just feel like talking, or interact with a virtual reality 3D model, Voxta is the way to go.

Host your own AI using Text Generation Web UI, KoboldAI and many others.

Connect to AI providers such as Open AI, OpenRouter, NovelAI and many others.

Features characters, summarization-based memory, prompt template customization and much more.
